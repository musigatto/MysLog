## MysLog
_MysLog_ is a free application designed to track your workouts. 
With this application, you can:
- Create and manage training **sessions**.
- Select exercises from a catalogue of more than **870** options, with advanced filters to facilitate your search.
- Consult detailed **instructions** and images for each exercise.
- Record exercises, **sets**, and **weight**.
- View **graphs** and statistics for each exercise.
- Save your entire training history **locally** and privately (100% _offline_). 

Although the entire application is designed to work in Spanish and English, please bear in mind that the database is currently only available in English.
